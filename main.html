ABOUT ME

I play games.

My name is Mitch King, but around the Internet I go by Woakzy. Video games haven't always been a part of my life. So first of all, I want to give some insight on who I am and how I came to have a passion to compete in esports.

At a young age I discovered Hockey. I still play today, and this team sport is where I developed my competitive spirit. This hunger to compete was only magnified when I first got introduced to esports and competitive gaming after stumbling upon large Twitch streamers like Ninja and TSM Myth. I saw them and thought of how cool it would be to stream like them when I got older, but then I started to see younger streamers appear and grow rapidly in popularity. After seeing the feedback and support from fans that younger and newer streamers received, I wanted to follow suit, but add my unique personality and style to the mix. Although I am young that doesn't limit me. In fact, I think it is beneficial that I've started younger as it allows me to build team and communication skills and become a better player and content creator. I am just beginning to make it into the public eye as I am competing in more tournaments and beginning my journey in esports as a legitimate competitor. My hope is to make it to a professional level.
